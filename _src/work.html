  • Online banking

    Client
    Major banking organization in Denmark
    Relation
    1508
    Type
    Online banking
    Role
    User Experience Designer
    Front-end Lead

    One of the most challenging projects I've ever been a part of, was at 1508. We were asked to reenvision how online banking works for consumers – focussing on mobile devices.

    The task was to create an entirely new experience that would leverage the available banking data to help consumers come to better grips with their banking affairs.

    We attacked the task in a very agile manner, with short sprints to test new interaction patterns and UX ideas through a rapidly updated prototype.

    Unfortunately, I'm not able to share any visuals from this project.

    Client

    Role

    Also during my time at Vertic, I helped Siemens develop an interactive, immersive 3D world in HTML5. The solution worked cross platform, and was adapted to both huge touch-screens, tablets and desktop computers.
