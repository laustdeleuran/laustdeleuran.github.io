  • Nars — Orgasm

    NARS Cosmetics was looking for a partner to develop a smooth and interactive scroll experience for their new cosmetics line, Orgasm. We was asked to help realize NARS' ambitions design vision within a tight timeline to match the launch dates across all media.

    We created a classic parallax experience with dynamic movement dependent on the user's own scroll speed. This leaves the user in control without stealing their scroll actions, while still showcasing the graphic elements of the site. Interspersed throughout the page is several full-page slides that create a natural rhythm as you scroll through the experience.

    The site contains several multi media and interactive elements, such as video, a blush color selector and wallpaper graphics for download.
