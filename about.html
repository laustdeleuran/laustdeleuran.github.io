Who are you even?

Relax. Don't panic. It's just a website. It's actually my website. I’m Laust and I’m a UX designer & front-end developer. So, just sit back and enjoy it. As much as you can enjoy a personal website, anyway.

Your website? You build these things?

Yes. I actually build websites for a living. I'm based in New York and I like to make stuff, especially when it's both effective and pretty. I've built websites since I was a mere 12 year old (back in 1997, when frames was still hot and <blink> was so cool).

Now, I spend most of my time working for Vertic, a digital advertising agency, where I do interaction design and front-end development. When I'm not working, I spend my time designing, sketching, coding half-finished web experiments and reading a lot of blog posts. Even though it's thoroughly nerdy I'm passionate about user interactions and code.

You can check out some of my more nerdy stuff on GitHub, JS Fiddle or Codepen. Or if you're already sold, here's my resume. Feel free to shoot me a mail at hello@ljd.dk, if you feel like it.
