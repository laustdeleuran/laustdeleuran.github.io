My work

I've worked on a lot of exciting projects for many different clients, from big corporations to small and local businesses. I love it all, as long as it provides a challenge!

Here's a few samples, but if you want to know more, don't hesitate to let me know:

  • Berlingske News Agency Telegram App

    Client
    Berlingske Media
    Type
    Corporate site
    Role
    Creative Technologist & Development Lead
    Preview link
    app.bnb.berlingskemedia.dev.ljd.dk

    Berlingske Media is one of the oldest and largest media agencies in Denmark. Originating in the newspaper business, Berlingske Media has a long history of producing journalism in a lot of different forms. One of their more recent products is a telegram service catering to large businesses and policy makers. The app delivers raw news as they emerge, often before the news hit mainstream media. The app also allows users to subscribe to subjects they find more interesting through push-notifications and allows berlingske to change the apps substructure as new journalistic themes emerge.

    To create a cohesive experience across a very diverse landscape of devices, the app was created using modern web technologies and packaged to four major device manufacturers.

  • Politiken - Bezzerwizzer

    Client
    Politiken
    Relation
    Umwelt
    Type
    Site component
    Role
    Front-end Lead
    Prototype
    bwquiz.preview.umwelt.dk

    Also for Umwelt, I've helped create a small new component for Danish newspaper Politiken. The component is a quiz (run by and with questions from the well-known game Bezzerwizzer. Users can participate in the quiz of the day as well as revisit earlier quizzes. Users logged in to the newspaper can also track their own statistics. The component works cross device and is fully fluid, even though Politiken's current uses an adaptive approach to serve different sites for desktop and mobile. This way, Politiken can reuse the same component across their platforms.

    The solution is built with AngularJS. All logic is done on the front-end as all data is provided through a simple REST API.
